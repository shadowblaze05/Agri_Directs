About Agri-Direct

Agri-Direct is designed to connect farmers, buyers, and administrators through a modern agricultural middleware platform.

With role-based access, intuitive dashboards, and analytics, Agri-Direct helps farmers manage harvest entries, buyers discover available crops, and administrators oversee the entire marketplace.

Features
  • Role-aware registration for farmers and buyers
  • Admin console for inventory and user management
  • Live dashboard analytics and crop comparison charts
  • Chat support with in-app AgriBot assistant
